Maximum Yield (MY) Checking
Open a Blue Ridge Bank MY Checking account and earn interest the easy way. With the MY Checking account, you earn premium interest on your balance by meeting a few simple requirements.

Features
  • Interest paid on all balances*
  • $100 to open
  • No minimum balance
  • Free online banking
  • Free online bill pay
  • Free mobile phone banking
  • Free Debit MasterCard® with digital wallet access
  • Unlimited withdrawals at MoneyPass® network ATMs per statement cycle - over 20,000 ATMs nationwide
  • Nationwide ATM fee refunds up to $25 per statement cycle when MY Checking qualifications are met.**
  • No monthly service charge

Requirements
Simply meet these requirements each monthly qualification cycle to earn premium interest:
  • Must be registered to receive electronic statements
  • Must have 12 or more debit or credit MasterCard® point-of-sale purchases
  • Must have one ACH direct deposit or payment set up
Also available: Maximum Yield (MY) Savings

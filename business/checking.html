Small Business Checking
At Blue Ridge Bank and Trust Co. we realize that as a small business you have unique needs.

We've designed our Small Business Checking Account just for you. The fees are less; the terms are just right.

Features include:
  • Low $5 monthly maintenance fee
  • Up to 75 deposited items per month
  • Up to 50 credits per month
  • Up to 50 debits per month

This account includes Business Online Cash Management to initiate account-to-account transfers, stop payments, download transaction activity and much more. Open this account for only $100.

  • Monitor activity on your account(s) daily
  • View account history up to 365 days
  • Initiate account-to-account transfers
  • Make loan payments
  • Place stop payments
  • Download transaction detail in various formats for easy reconcilement.

Regular Business Checking
This account allows business customers to earn a competitive earnings credit rate to help offset service fees based on your average collected balance. There are no transaction limits on this account. Contact us to learn more about account service fees. Open this account for only $100.

Business Green Checking

This account offers everything you need for FREE.

Make the most of your business and save green by going green! Enjoy more convenient banking while helping the environment. Business Green Checking is an all-electronic account designed for companies with low activity.

Features include:

  • No monthly maintenance fee
  • No minimum balance
  • Free Business Debit MasterCard®
  • Free Business Online Cash Management
  • Free e-Statements
  • Free online check images
  • Free 24-hour Telephone Banking
  • 50 Free items per month*
  • Unlimited Debit Card POS purchases*

You must deposit $100 to open this account. This account is all electronic and requires an active email account and enrollment in NetTeller online banking. View the Deposit Account Agreement (PDF).

*Includes deposits, deposited items and debits. Debit card POS (point of sale) purchases are unlimited and do not count against the 50 free items per month. Paper statements are available for a $5 monthly fee. A charge of $25 will be assessed on the account if closed within 180 days from the date of opening.
Not-for-Profit Checking
Blue Ridge Bank offers a business checking account designed for our Not-for-Profit customers.

Features include:

  • No monthly maintenance fee that allows for 50 debits, 50 credits, 75 deposited items
  • Low opening balance - $100
  • Limited check writing
  • Image copies of checks
  • Debit Business Card

As an added feature, you will receive Business Online Cash Management for FREE - a $15 per month value! With this service, you can:
  • Monitor activity on your account(s) daily
  • View account history up to 365 days
  • Initiate account-to-account transfers
  • Make loan payments
  • Place stop payments
  • Download transaction detail in various formats for easy reconcilement.

At least one signer must have a personal bank account relationship to be eligible for this account. If there is no personal relationship, the customer may open a small business checking account. There is a $0.25 per item fee over allotted transaction limit.

View our Deposit Account Agreement (PDF).
